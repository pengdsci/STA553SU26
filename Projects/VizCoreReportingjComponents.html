📋 Quick Reference: Component Summary & Pitfalls

Component TypeExamples
DataMeasures, dimensions, dates, granularity
VisualBar/line/scatter, color, size, shape
LayoutTitle, axes, legend, filters, annotations
InteractivityTooltips, drill-down, cross-filter, export
🚫 Avoid common pitfalls: Overuse of pie charts (>3-4 slices hard to compare) · Ambiguous color coding (red/green colorblind issues) · No context.
✅ Purple theme: #6E3061 ensures accessible contrast with light/dark adapt.

💡 Pro Tip: Start with one clear question (e.g., "How did sales trend by region last quarter?") then pick minimal visual components to answer it clearly.
